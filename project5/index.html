Music Discovery Journey

Are you interested in listening to bands you've probably never heard of? Allow me to "put you on" to some of my favorite bands and bands I've discovered while doing this project.

You've made it this far down, so you MUST be interested in discovering new bands. We will cover two bands on this page that are drastically different from each other.

Why should you listen to these bands and what might you gain from them?

There is plenty you can attain from finding new music:

  • Connection with new cultures
  • Reduced Stress
  • Better Memory
  • Fosters personal growth
  • Improved Cognitive Skills
  • Social Opportunities

Let's take a look at those bands:

Big Thief

Pictured above is Big Thief, an American indie folk band formed in Brooklyn, New York in the year 2015. From left to right, is Buck Meek, James Kricvchenia, Adrianne Lenker, and Max Oleartchik (no longer in the group)

Gojira

A personal favorite of mine, Gojira, a French heavy metal band from the Ondres. Originally known as Godzilla in 1996, they rebranded to Gojira in 2001. Their members include from left to right, Mario Duplaintier, Christian Andreu, Joe Duplaintier, and Jean-Michel Labadie.
