Inspirations and Takeaways

Different music invokes different feelings. This page highlights who inspired both bands and what their listener's take away from listening to them.

Gojira

Gojira, the French Metal Band we've taken a fair dive into, draws their inspirations from other metal and progressive bands like:

  • Sepultera
  • Morbid Angel
  • Death

They also implement French philosophy mixed with Buddhist ideas and drown it into their music, painting their music sheet canvas with themes of environmentalism and spirituality.

What listeners take home:

  • A strong understanding of existential and environmental issues
  • Spiritual reflection
  • Philosophical Reflection
  • Catharsis, through intensity

Big Thief

Big Thief, the indie-folk band we have discussed, draws their inspiration from artists including:

  • Bob Dylan
  • Neil Young
  • Joni Mitchell

You can clearly pick up the somber, yet brutal tones of Elliott Smith, and the subtle, yet crushing chord progressions of Sharon Van Etten.

What fans take away:

  • Intimacy and Connection
  • Understanding of Emotions
  • Understanding of Vulnerability
  • Comfort and relatedness

I certainly hope you enjoyed my deep dive on my two very drastically different bands. In my opinion, by listening to them you will gain a deeper appreciation for life, and what it means to appreciate every moment for what it is. Each band offers a different perspective, and what you choose to take out of the band as the listener is completely up to interpretation, but I definitely urge you to take a listen.
